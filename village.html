Two Realms, One
Unforgettable Journey

Ghoomar Village is an all-inclusive ticketed experience where the celebration never stops. Your evening spans two distinctive areas carefully crafted to transport you straight to the heart of Rajasthan.

The Village Gully
A lively street bursting with carnival games, live pottery, mehendi artisans, and aromatic street-food stalls serving hot chaats and authentic snacks.

The Evergreen Classics

Dal Baati Churma

The soul of Rajasthan. Hard wheat rolls baked to perfection, served with spicy lentil curry and sweet crumbled wheat.
